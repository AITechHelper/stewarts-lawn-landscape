FAQ

Questions We Hear All the Time

Here are quick answers to what Tulsa homeowners ask us most. Don't see your question? Just call — we're always happy to talk it through.

Do you offer free estimates?▾
Yes — always. We'll come out, walk the property with you, and give you a straight quote at no charge. No obligation, no pressure.
What areas do you serve?▾
We serve Tulsa and the surrounding communities including Broken Arrow, Bixby, Jenks, Owasso, Sand Springs, Sapulpa, and more. Call us if you're unsure — we'll let you know right away.
Can you handle one-time jobs or just recurring service?▾
Both. We handle one-time cleanups, seasonal projects, and full landscaping installs just as readily as we take on regular weekly or biweekly lawn maintenance. Whatever you need, we can help.
Do you repair existing irrigation systems?▾
Absolutely. We install new irrigation systems and repair or service existing ones. Whether you've got a broken head, a valve issue, or a system that needs a full inspection, we'll get it sorted.
How often should my lawn be mowed?▾
Most Tulsa lawns benefit from weekly mowing during the growing season (spring through early fall) and every other week as growth slows down. We'll recommend the right frequency for your specific grass type and yard.
When is the best time to start a landscaping project?▾
Spring and fall are ideal for most landscaping work in Tulsa — temperatures are mild and plants establish well. That said, we work year-round, so reach out anytime and we'll advise on the best timing for your specific project.
Are you licensed and insured?▾
Yes. We are fully licensed and insured, giving you complete peace of mind every time we're on your property. Your home is in good hands with Stewarts.
How do I get started?▾
Just call or text us at (918) 748-9429, or fill out the contact form below. We'll respond quickly, set up a time to come look at your property, and get you a free quote — usually within 24 hours.
